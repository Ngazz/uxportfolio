Background

M-PESA is a mobile money transaction platform launched in 2008 by a giant Telecomunication Company In Kenya, Safaricom. By the end of 2023, it had over 36million users across Kenya.

Overview

Despite M-PESA being a successful moble money transfer platform, its reliance on mobile devices exposes the mobile users to threats of losing such devices to muggers and other criminals.

Problem is that ...

People relying on mobile phones for cashless transactions become a target by criminals and muggers. A report published by The Star Newspaper 12 Dec, 2022 shows that atleast one person loses a mobile phone in every 10 Minutes.
