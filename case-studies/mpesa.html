M-PESA Smart

Case Study

Duration

This is a Solo Three week Design Sprint.

From:10-01-2024 To:Ongoing

Overview

Addressing the rising threat of mobile loss in Kenya, particularly targeting individuals engaged in frequent mobile money transactions.

Goal

Provide a secure and User-friendly solution that provide a Seemless & Convinient Cashless transactions to those relying on them.

My Role

  • Ux Research
  • UI Design
  • Userbility Testing

Research

I was curious to understand how loosing mobile phones impacted the overal money transaction made by people. So I conducted round up of interviews, including surveing a large pool of people relying on mobile money transactions as well as people who at one point lost their mobile phones.

Why Smartwatch App?

Evolved from being just a mere device showing time, to finding space in fashion; Smartwaches Can be a great tool to aid in cashless transactions. The chances of losing them is bare minimum.

User Testing

Recruited 8 participants and keenly monitored their journey in fulfilling some tasks. This led to further improvement and settling on this design.

Methodology

In person Testing;
15th-30th January 2024.

Participants Category

  • 15 yrs- 20yrs=2
  • 21 yrs- 30yrs=4
  • 31 yrs- 40yrs=1
  • Above 41 yrs=1

Opportunity

"It gets boring to have to start again the process when the user makes a mistake; can there be a way to process & verify a transaction before inputing amount and authorization particulars (PIN or Fingerprint)?" ‐ Lucy.17-01-2024

Looking Back
